Bath has a rich literary history with books written in Bath, inspired by Bath, and published in Bath - as well as tales of different experiences when famous names have visited this world heritage city. This site creates a physical and historical trail around the city, providing digital content to aid in discovery of these stories.

Charles Dickens, Mary Shelley, Jane Austen - they all have experiences of the city to be discovered. Hunt out the posters around Bath libraries and interact with your mobile device to obtain further digital content.
